# CityFlow Discord Server Rules

## Official Rules

### Rule 1: Be Respectful
**Description:** Treat all members with respect. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
**Examples:**
- Personal attacks or insults
- Discriminatory language
- Targeted harassment
- Threats of violence
**Penalty:** Warning → Mute → Kick → Ban

### Rule 2: No Spamming
**Description:** Avoid flooding channels, excessive messages, or self-promotion without permission.
**Examples:**
- Sending 5+ messages in quick succession
- Posting the same message repeatedly
- Excessive caps lock usage
- Unsolicited self-promotion
**Penalty:** Warning → Mute → Kick → Ban

### Rule 3: Stay On Topic
**Description:** Keep discussions in the appropriate channels.
**Examples:**
- Game discussion in #general-chat
- Off-topic in #cityflow-discussion
- Support requests in #general-chat
**Penalty:** Verbal warning → Written warning

### Rule 4: No NSFW Content
**Description:** Keep the server family-friendly.
**Examples:**
- Sexual content
- Graphic violence
- Disturbing imagery
- Inappropriate language
**Penalty:** Mute → Kick → Ban

### Rule 5: Follow Discord ToS
**Description:** Adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
**Examples:**
- Illegal activity
- Malware distribution
- Account selling
- Raiding
**Penalty:** Immediate ban

### Rule 6: Report Bugs
**Description:** Don't exploit bugs. Report them in #bug-reports.
**Examples:**
- Using game glitches for advantage
- Sharing exploits publicly
- Not reporting known bugs
**Penalty:** Warning → Mute → Ban

### Rule 7: Privacy First
**Description:** Don't share personal information of other members.
**Examples:**
- Sharing real names without consent
- Posting addresses or phone numbers
- Sharing private conversations
- Doxxing
**Penalty:** Ban → Report to Discord

### Rule 8: Listen to Staff
**Description:** Follow staff decisions. DM them for disputes.
**Examples:**
- Arguing with staff publicly
- Ignoring staff instructions
- Refusing to comply
**Penalty:** Warning → Mute → Kick → Ban

### Rule 9: No Alts
**Description:** Don't use alternate accounts to evade bans or mutes.
**Examples:**
- Creating new accounts after ban
- Using friend's accounts
- Sharing accounts
**Penalty:** Ban all associated accounts

### Rule 10: Have Fun!
**Description:** Enjoy the CityFlow community!
**Examples:**
- Participate in events
- Help new players
- Share achievements
- Be positive

## Verification Rules

### Verification Process
1. **Read Rules** - Understand all server rules
2. **React** - Click the checkmark reaction in #rules
3. **Receive Role** - Get Verified Player role
4. **Access Channels** - Gain access to community channels

### Verification Requirements
- **Age:** 13+ years old
- **Account:** Verified email address
- **Behavior:** No recent violations
- **Intent:** Genuine interest in CityFlow

## Moderation Actions

### Warning System
1. **Verbal Warning** - First offense
2. **Written Warning** - Second offense
3. **Mute** - Third offense
4. **Kick** - Fourth offense
5. **Ban** - Fifth offense

### Mute Durations
- **1 hour** - Minor offenses
- **6 hours** - Moderate offenses
- **24 hours** - Serious offenses
- **7 days** - Severe offenses

### Ban Policy
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Appeal:** Contact staff
- **Review:** Within 48 hours

## Staff Guidelines

### Moderation Principles
1. **Fairness** - Treat everyone equally
2. **Consistency** - Apply rules consistently
3. **Transparency** - Explain actions clearly
4. **Proportionality** - Match punishment to offense

### Staff Commands
- **Warning:** /warn @user [reason]
- **Mute:** /mute @user [duration] [reason]
- **Kick:** /kick @user [reason]
- **Ban:** /ban @user [reason]

### Escalation
- **Helper** → **Moderator**
- **Moderator** → **Senior Moderator**
- **Senior Moderator** → **Community Manager**
- **Community Manager** → **Administrator**

## Appeals Process

### How to Appeal
1. **DM Staff** - Contact a moderator
2. **Explain** - Provide context and reasoning
3. **Wait** - Staff will review within 48 hours
4. **Outcome** - Decision communicated to you

### Appeal Guidelines
- **Be honest** - Provide accurate information
- **Be respectful** - Don't argue with staff
- **Be patient** - Reviews take time
- **Accept decision** - Staff decisions are final

## Emergency Procedures

### Raid Response
1. **Lock** channels immediately
2. **Ban** raid accounts
3. **Alert** senior staff
4. **Review** security

### Doxxing Response
1. **Delete** content
2. **Ban** user
3. **Report** to Discord
4. **Alert** affected users

### Threat Response
1. **Ban** user immediately
2. **Report** to authorities
3. **Document** evidence
4. **Alert** staff
